Farmer Information

Welcome to FarmEASY Portal!

FarmEASY Portal provides a single window for farmers to access information about various loan schemes provided by banks and make applications for Loans.

If you want to apply for a loan on FarmEASY portal, you must register on the portal. Please provide essential details as mentioned in the registration form.

Important Instructions

  • Ensure that all the details entered in the registration form are accurate and correct.
  • Your password must meet the required security format.
  • Double-check your email ID before submitting, as it cannot be changed later.

Guidelines for Registering

  • Name: Enter your name exactly as it appears on your Aadhaar/PAN Card or any other document attached to your loan application.
  • Mobile Number: Provide a valid mobile number.
  • Email ID: Enter a valid personal email ID. All necessary communications, updates, and alerts will be sent to this email.

Need Help?

If you face any difficulties during the registration process, you can refer to the FAQs section or contact our support team for assistance.
